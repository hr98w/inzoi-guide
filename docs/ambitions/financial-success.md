# Financial Success
Secure sufficient monetary resources to live without financial worries
## Wealth
### Level 1
- Buy 5 pieces of Furniture worth over 800 Meow
- Hold 50000 Meow

### Level 2
- Deposit money 10 time(s) at the bank
- Reside at a property priced over 35000 Meow

### Level 3
- Hold 200000 Meow
- Reside at a property priced over 50000 Meow


## Luxury Mansion
### Level 1
- Hold 50000 Meow
- Reside at a property priced over 35000 Meow

### Level 2
- Buy 1 piece of furniture worth over 1500 Meow
- Plant 10 medium-sized or larger tree(s)

### Level 3
- Buy furniture worth over 800 Meow 5 time(s)
- Reside at a property priced over 50000 Meow
